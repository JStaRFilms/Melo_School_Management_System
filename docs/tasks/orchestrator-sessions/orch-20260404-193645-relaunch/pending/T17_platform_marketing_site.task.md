# T17 Platform Marketing Website

## Objective

Create a separate marketing website for the SchoolOS product itself so schools can discover, evaluate, and contact the platform team before they ever reach a tenant-specific school public site.

## Why This Exists

The current `apps/www` work under `T10` is school-facing:
- it represents one school's public website
- it is tailored to prospective families and admissions intent
- it should remain tenant-brandable

That is different from the platform's own product-marketing presence, which needs to explain:
- what the SchoolOS product is
- what modules/features it offers
- who it is for
- how schools can book a demo or contact the platform team

## Requested Scope

- create a distinct platform-marketing surface separate from tenant school `apps/www`
- position the product for school owners and operators, not parents
- cover product overview, feature groups, pricing/packaging teaser, demo/contact CTA, and trust signals
- keep the platform brand separate from school-specific branding
- align with future `T12` pricing/packaging strategy instead of inventing conflicting commercial copy

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] A distinct platform-marketing site exists separately from the tenant school public website.
- [ ] The platform site speaks to school decision-makers rather than families.
- [ ] Product-level CTAs exist for demo/contact intent.
- [ ] The platform site does not get conflated with tenant school branding or admissions content.
- [ ] The content leaves room for future pricing/packaging work from `T12`.

## Notes

- This should remain separate from `T10`.
- Do not merge platform-product marketing copy into the tenant school site.
- Coordinate eventual pricing language with `T12`.
