# T11 Household Management Hardening

## Objective

Tighten the parent/family administration surface after the foundation work and portal bridge are in place.

## Requested Scope

- safe parent email maintenance after provisioning mistakes
- clear duplicate-link review when a school admin enters a parent email that already belongs to an existing parent/household
- child-to-parent unlink/edit flows that preserve household integrity
- clearer household-level vs student-level destructive actions

## Why This Exists

The current foundation supports:
- family linking
- portal credential provisioning
- student unlink from household

But the school still needs deeper household management controls before final release-hardening.

## Notes

Keep this as a dedicated follow-up slice so billing, portal, and family integrity rules are designed together rather than patched ad hoc.
