## Objective

Define the visual direction for a reusable school product that can feel premium for one school now and still adapt cleanly to later tenants.

## Scope

Included: design principles, mood, theming variables, school-brand override rules.  
Excluded: final coded components.

## Context

This brief informs all mockups for `www`, `admin`, `teacher`, and `portal`.

## Definition of Done

- A clear brand brief exists.
- White-label rules explain what changes per school and what stays platform-consistent.

## Expected Artifacts

- `docs/design/brand-brief.md`

## Constraints

- Avoid generic SaaS visuals.
- Keep mobile-first readability and school trustworthiness central.

## Verification

- Confirm the brief supports both platform consistency and school branding overrides.
