# [FR-007] Results Entry and Moderation

## Labels
`MUS`, `results`, `workflow`

## User Story
As a teacher or admin, I want controlled score entry, editing, and approval workflows, so that published results are accurate and auditable.

## Proposed Solution

### Overview
Separate draft score entry from final publication, with change tracking, approval checkpoints, and role-based editing rights.

### Implementation Flow
1. Add result-entry states such as draft, submitted, approved, published.
2. Record who entered or changed scores.
3. Prevent unauthorized edits to published records.

### Key Considerations
- Teachers and admins need different edit windows.
- Auditability is required for grade changes.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Result records support workflow states.
- [ ] Grade changes record actor and timestamp.
- [ ] Published results are protected from casual editing.
